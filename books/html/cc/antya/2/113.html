TEXT 113
'āji haite ei mora ājñā pālibā
choṭa haridāse ihāṅ āsite nā dibā'
SYNONYMS
āji haite—from today; ei—this; mora—My; ājñā—order; pālibā—you should carry out; choṭa haridāse—Choṭa Haridāsa; ihāṅ—here; āsite—to come; nā dibā—do not allow.
TRANSLATION
" From this day forward, do not allow Choṭa Haridāsa to come here."
