TEXT 132
more ājñā haya, muñi yāṅa ālālanātha
ekale rahiba tāhāṅ, govinda-mātra sātha"
SYNONYMS
more—to Me; ājñā haya—please give permission; muñi—I; yāṅa—go; ālālanātha—to the place known as Ālālanātha; ekale rahiba—I shall remain alone; tāhāṅ—there; govinda-mātra sātha—with only Govinda.
TRANSLATION
"Please give Me permission to go to Ālālanātha. I shall remain there alone; only Govinda will go with Me."
