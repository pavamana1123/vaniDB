TEXT 133
śrīdhara-svāmī-prasāde 'bhāgavata' jāni
jagad-guru śrīdhara-svāmī 'guru' kari' māni
SYNONYMS
śrīdhara-svāmī—of Śrīdhara Svāmī; prasāde—by the mercy; bhāgavata jāni—we can understand Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam; jagat-guru—the spiritual master of the entire world; śrīdhara-svāmī—Śrīdhara Svāmī; guru kari'-as a spiritual master; māni—I accept.
TRANSLATION
"Śrīdhara Svāmī is the spiritual master of the entire world because by his mercy we can understand Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam. I therefore accept him as a spiritual master.
