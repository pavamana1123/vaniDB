TEXT 174
aneka paṇḍita sabhāya, brāhmaṇa, sajjana
dui bhāi mahā-paṇḍita--hiraṇya, govardhana
SYNONYMS
aneka paṇḍita—many learned scholars; sabhāya—in that assembly; brāhmaṇa—brāhmaṇas; sat-jana—respectable gentlemen; dui bhāi—the two brothers; mahā-paṇḍita—very learned scholars; hiraṇya—Hiraṇya; govardhana—Govardhana.
TRANSLATION
In that assembly were many learned scholars, brāhmaṇas and respectable gentlemen. The two brothers Hiraṇya and Govardhana were also greatly learned.
