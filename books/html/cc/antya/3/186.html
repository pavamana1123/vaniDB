TEXT 186
mukti' tuccha-phala haya nāmābhāsa haite
SYNONYMS
'mukti—liberation; tuccha-phala—insignificant result; haya—is; nāma-ābhāsa haite—from a glimpse of awakening of offenseless chanting of the holy name.
TRANSLATION
"Liberation is the insignificant result derived from a glimpse of awakening of offenseless chanting of the holy name.
