TEXT 130
kīrtana karite aiche rātri-śeṣa haila
ṭhākurera sane veśyāra mana phiri' gela
SYNONYMS
kīrtana karite—chanting and chanting; aiche—in that way; rātri-śeṣa haila—the night ended; ṭhākurera sane—by the association of Haridāsa Ṭhākura; veśyāra—of the prostitute; mana—mind; phiri' gela—was converted.
TRANSLATION
The night ended while Haridāsa Ṭhākura was chanting, but by his association the mind of the prostitute had changed.
