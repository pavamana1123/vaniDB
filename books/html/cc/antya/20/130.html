TEXT 130
mahā-prasādera tāhāṅ mahimā varṇilā
kṛṣṇādharāmṛtera phala-śloka āsvādilā
SYNONYMS
mahā-prasādera—of the remnants of the food of the Lord, mahā-prasāda; tāhāṅ—there; mahimā—the glories; varṇilā—described; kṛṣṇa-adhara-amṛtera—of the nectar from the lips of Kṛṣṇa; phala-śloka—the verse mentioning the result; āsvādilā—tasted.
TRANSLATION
Also in that chapter, the glories of mahā-prasāda are explained, and a verse is tasted describing the effect of nectar from the lips of Kṛṣṇa.
