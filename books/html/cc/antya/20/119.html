TEXT 119
ekādaśe--haridāsa-ṭhākurera niryāṇa
bhakta-vātsalya yāhāṅ dekhāilā gaura bhagavān
SYNONYMS
ekādaśe—in the Eleventh Chapter; haridāsa-ṭhākurera niryāṇa—the disappearance of Śrīla Haridāsa Ṭhākura; bhakta-vātsalya—affection for the devotee; yāhāṅ—wherein; dekhāilā—exhibited; gaura bhagavān—Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the Supreme Personality of Godhead.
TRANSLATION
The Eleventh Chapter describes the disappearance of Haridāsa Ṭhākura and how Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, showed His affection for His devotees.
