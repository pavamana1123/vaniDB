TEXT 122
śrīmān-sena, śrīmān-paṇḍita, ācārya-nandana
tāṅ-sabāra datta ei karaha bhojana
SYNONYMS
śrīmān-sena—Śivānanda Sena; śrīmān-paṇḍita—Śrīmān Paṇḍita; ācārya-nandana—Ācārya Nandana; tāṅ-sabāra—of all of them; datta—given; ei—these; karaha bhojana—please eat.
TRANSLATION
"These are gifts given by Śrīmān Sena, Śrīmān Paṇḍita and Ācārya Nandana. Please eat them all.
