TEXT 68
"jagamohana-pari-muṇḍā yāu"
SYNONYMS
jagamohana—the kīrtana hall known as Jagamohana; pari—in; muṇḍā—my head; yāu—let it be offered.
TRANSLATION
"Let my head fall at the feet of Jagannātha in the kīrtana hall known as Jagamohana."
