TEXT 114
aneka 'sukṛte' ihā hañāche samprāpti
sabe ei āsvāda kara kari' mahā-bhakti"
SYNONYMS
aneka—many; sukṛte—by pious activities; ihā—this; hañāche samprāpti—has become available; sabe—all of you; ei—this prasāda; āsvāda kara—taste; kari' mahā-bhakti—with great devotion.
TRANSLATION
"This prasāda has been made available only as a result of many pious activities. Now taste it with great faith and devotion."
