TEXT 101
ei-mata bahu-bāra kīrtana karite
'hari-bola' bali' prabhu uṭhe ācambite
SYNONYMS
ei-mata—in this way; bahu-bāra—for a long time; kīrtana karite—chanting; hari-bola bali'-uttering Hari bol; prabhu—Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; uṭhe—stands up; ācambite—suddenly.
TRANSLATION
After the devotees had been chanting for a long time, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu suddenly stood up, shouting, "Hari bol!"
