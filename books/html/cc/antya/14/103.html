TEXT 103
uṭhi' mahāprabhu vismita, iti uti cāya
ye dekhite cāya, tāhā dekhite nā pāya
SYNONYMS
uṭhi'-standing up; mahāprabhu—Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; vismita—astonished; iti uti—here and there; cāya—looks; ye—what; dekhite cāya—He wanted to see; tāhā—that; dekhite nā pāya—He could not see.
TRANSLATION
Astonished, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu stood up and began looking here and there, trying to see something. But He could not catch sight of it.
