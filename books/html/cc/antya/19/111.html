TEXT 111
caitanya-caritāmṛta--nitya-nūtana
śunite śunite juḍāya hṛdaya-śravaṇa
SYNONYMS
caitanya-carita-amṛta—the book named Caitanya-caritāmṛta; nitya-nūtana—always fresh; śunite śunite—by hearing and hearing; juḍāya—becomes pacified; hṛdaya-śravaṇa—the ear and heart.
TRANSLATION
Caitanya-caritāmṛta is ever-increasingly fresh. Continuously hearing it pacifies one's heart and ear.
