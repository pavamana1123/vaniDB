TEXT 106
"mitaṁ ca sāraṁ ca vaco hi vāgmitā" iti
SYNONYMS
mitam—concise; ca—and; sāram—essential; ca—and; vacaḥ—speech; hi—certainly; vāgmitā—eloquence; iti—thus.
TRANSLATION
"Essential truth spoken concisely is true eloquence."
