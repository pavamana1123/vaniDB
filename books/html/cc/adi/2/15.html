TEXT 15
koṭī koṭī brahmāṇḍe ye brahmera vibhūti
sei brahma govindera haya aṅga-kānti
SYNONYMS
koṭī—tens of millions; koṭī—tens of millions; brahma-aṇḍe—in universes; ye—which; brahmera—of Brahman; vibhūti—opulences; sei—that; brahma—Brahman; govindera—of Lord Govinda; haya—is; aṅga-kānti—bodily effulgence.
TRANSLATION
"The opulences of the impersonal Brahman spread throughout the millions and millions of universes. That Brahman is but the bodily effulgence of Govinda.
