TEXT 40
jīvera īśvara--puruṣādi avatāra
tāṅhā sabā haite tomāra aiśvarya apāra
SYNONYMS
jīvera—of the living beings; īśvara—the Supreme Lord; puruṣa-ādi—puruṣa incarnations, etc.; avatāra—incarnations; tāṅhā—Them; sabā—all; haite—than; tomāra—Your; aiśvarya—opulences; apāra—boundless.
TRANSLATION
"The direct Lords of the living beings are the puruṣa incarnations. But Your opulence and power are more exalted than Theirs.
