TEXT 211
śrī-madana-gopāla-śrī-govinda-daraśana
kahibāra yogya nahe e-saba kathana
SYNONYMS
śrī-madana-gopāla—Lord Madana Gopāla; śrī-govinda—Lord Rādhā-Govinda; daraśana—visiting; kahibāra—to speak; yogya—fit; nahe—not; e-saba kathana—all these confidential words.
TRANSLATION
I am not fit to speak all these confidential words about my visiting Lord Madana Gopāla and Lord Govinda.
