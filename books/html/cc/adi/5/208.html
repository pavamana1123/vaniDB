TEXT 208
preme matta nityānanda kṛpā-avatāra
uttama, adhama, kichu nā kare vicāra
SYNONYMS
preme—in ecstatic love; matta—mad; nityānanda—Lord Nityānanda; kṛpā—merciful; avatāra—incarnation; uttama—good; adhama—bad; kichu—any; nā—not; kare—makes; vicāra—consideration.
TRANSLATION
Because He is intoxicated by ecstatic love and is an incarnation of mercy, He does not distinguish between the good and the bad.
