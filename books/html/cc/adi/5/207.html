TEXT 207
emana nirghṛṇa more kebā kṛpā kare
eka nityānanda vinu jagat bhitare
SYNONYMS
emana—such; nirghṛṇa—abominable; more—unto me; kebā—who; kṛpā—mercy; kare—shows; eka—one; nityānanda—Lord Nityānanda; vinu—but; jagat—world; bhitare—within.
TRANSLATION
Who in this world but Nityānanda could show His mercy to such an abominable person as me?
