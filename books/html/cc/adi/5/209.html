TEXT 209
ye āge paḍaye, tāre karaye nistāra
ataeva nistārilā mo-hena durācāra
SYNONYMS
ye—whoever; āge—in front; paḍaye—falls down; tāre—unto him; karaye—does; nistāra—deliverance; ataeva—therefore; nistārilā—delivered; mo—as me; hena—such; durācāra—sinful and fallen person.
TRANSLATION
He delivers all those who fall down before Him. Therefore He has delivered such a sinful and fallen person as me.
