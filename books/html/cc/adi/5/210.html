TEXT 210
mo-pāpiṣṭhe ānilena śrī-vṛndāvana
mo-hena adhame dilā śrī-rūpa-caraṇa
SYNONYMS
mo-pāpiṣṭhe—unto me, who am so sinful; ānilena—He brought; śrī-vṛndāvana—to Vṛndāvana; mo-hena—such as me; adhame—to the lowest of mankind; dilā—delivered; śrī-rūpa-caraṇa—the lotus feet of Rūpa Gosvāmī.
TRANSLATION
Although I am sinful and I am the most fallen, He has conferred upon me the lotus feet of Śrī Rūpa Gosvāmī.
