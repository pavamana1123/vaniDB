TEXT 195
āre āre kṛṣṇadāsa, nā karaha bhaya
vṛndāvane yāha,--tāṅhā sarva labhya haya
SYNONYMS
āre āre—O! O!; kṛṣṇa-dāsa—of the name Kṛṣṇadāsa; nā—not; karaha—make; bhaya—fear; vṛndāvane yāha—go to Vṛndāvana; tāṅhā—there; sarva—everything; labhya—available; haya—is.
TRANSLATION
"O my dear Kṛṣṇadāsa, do not be afraid. Go to Vṛndāvana, for there you will attain all things."
