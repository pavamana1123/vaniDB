TEXT 200
jaya jaya nityānanda, nityānanda-rāma
yāṅhāra kṛpāte pāinu vṛndāvana-dhāma
SYNONYMS
jaya jaya—all glories; nityānanda—to Lord Nityānanda; nityānanda-rāma—to Lord Balarāma, who appeared as Nityānanda; yāṅhāra kṛpāte—by whose mercy; pāinu—I got; vṛndāvana-dhāma—shelter at Vṛndāvana.
TRANSLATION
All glory, all glory to Lord Nityānanda Balarāma, by whose mercy I have attained shelter in the transcendental abode of Vṛndāvana.
