TEXT 206
mora nāma śune yei tāra puṇya kṣaya
mora nāma laya yei tāra pāpa haya
SYNONYMS
mora nāma—my name; śune—hears; yei—anyone who; tāra—his; puṇya kṣaya—destruction of piety; mora nāma—my name; laya—takes; yei—anyone; tāra—his; pāpa—sin; haya—is.
TRANSLATION
Anyone who hears my name loses the results of his pious activities. Anyone who utters my name becomes sinful.
