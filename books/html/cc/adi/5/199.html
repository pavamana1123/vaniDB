TEXT 199
sei kṣaṇe vṛndāvane karinu gamana
prabhura kṛpāte sukhe āinu vṛndāvana
SYNONYMS
sei kṣaṇe—that very second; vṛndāvane—toward Vṛndāvana; karinu—I did; gamana—starting; prabhura kṛpāte—by the mercy of Lord Nityānanda; sukhe—in great happiness; āinu—arrived; vṛndāvana—at Vṛndāvana.
TRANSLATION
That very second I started for Vṛndāvana, and by His mercy I reached there in great happiness.
