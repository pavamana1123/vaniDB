TEXT 107
anyera āchuk kārya, āpane śrī-kṛṣṇa
āpana-mādhurya-pāne ha-ilā satṛṣṇa
SYNONYMS
anyera—of others; āchuk—let be; kārya—the business; āpane—personally; śrī-kṛṣṇa—Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa; āpana-mādhurya—personal sweetness; pāne—in drinking; ha-ilā—became; sa-tṛṣṇa—much eager.
TRANSLATION
What to speak of others, even Lord Kṛṣṇa Himself becomes thirsty to taste His own sweetness.
