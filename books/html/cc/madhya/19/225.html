TEXT 225
'mamatā' adhika, kṛṣṇe ātma-sama jñāna
ataeva sakhya-rasera vaśa bhagavān
SYNONYMS
mamatā—intimacy; adhika—increase; kṛṣṇe—with Kṛṣṇa; ātma-sama jñāna—the notion of equality; ataeva—therefore; sakhya-rasera—by the mellow of fraternity; vaśa—subjected; bhagavān—the Supreme Personality of Godhead.
TRANSLATION
"On the platform of sakhya-rasa, the Supreme Personality of Godhead is obliged to the devotees who are intimate with Kṛṣṇa and think themselves equal to Him.
