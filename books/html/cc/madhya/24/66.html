TEXT 66
tathāpi ca-kārera kahe mukhya artha sāta
SYNONYMS
tathāpi—still; ca-kārera—of the word ca; kahe—it is said; mukhya—chief; artha—meanings; sāta—seven.
TRANSLATION
"The word ca can be explained in seven ways.
