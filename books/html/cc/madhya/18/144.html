TEXT 144
'soro-kṣetre, āge yāñā kari' gaṅgā-snāna
sei pathe prabhu lañā kariye payāna
SYNONYMS
soro-kṣetre—to the holy place named Soro-kṣetra; āge—first, beyond; yāñā—going; kari' gaṅgā-snāna—having taken bath in the Ganges; sei pathe—that way; prabhu lañā—taking Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kariye payāna—let us go.
TRANSLATION
"After going to the holy place named Soro-kṣetra, and taking bath in the Ganges, let us take Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu that way and go.
