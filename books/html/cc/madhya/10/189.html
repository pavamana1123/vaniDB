TEXT 189
ei ta' kahila prabhura vaiṣṇava-milana
ihā yei śune, pāya caitanya-caraṇa
SYNONYMS
ei ta'-thus; kahila—I have described; prabhura—of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; vaiṣṇava-milana—meeting with all the Vaiṣṇavas; ihā—this narration; yei—anyone who; śune—hears; pāya—gets; caitanya-caraṇa—the shelter of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
TRANSLATION
Thus I have described the meeting of all the Vaiṣṇavas with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Whoever hears this description ultimately attains His shelter.
