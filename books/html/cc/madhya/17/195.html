TEXT 195
gābhī dekhi' stabdha prabhu premera taraṅge
vātsalye gābhī prabhura cāṭe saba-aṅge
SYNONYMS
gābhī dekhi'-seeing the cows; stabdha—stunned; prabhu—Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; premera taraṅge—in the waves of ecstatic love; vātsalye—in great affection; gābhī—all the cows; prabhura—of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; cāṭe—licked; saba-aṅge—all over the body.
TRANSLATION
Seeing the herds approach Him, the Lord was stunned with ecstatic love. The cows then began to lick His body in great affection.
